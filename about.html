About us

ARDI was founded by Diana and Artem, born from a deep love for ramen and Japanese cuisine. Our journey took us to Japan, where we explored the rich flavors and traditions of authentic ramen, bringing the best tastes from our trip back to our restaurant.

Why ramen? Because ramen is love. Ramen is home. Ramen is ARDI.
